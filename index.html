We decided on making a simualure webapplication than CookieClicker but we wanted to use a cake instead of a Cookie.
We want to have a cake that is clickable and several updates that when bought add on to the cake per second (Cps). We also
Want to have a cosmatic shob that allows you to buy different Cake-Skins.
Here is the List of features we want to have implimented

  1. A Cake that can be clicked
  2. A upgrade feature on the right where you can click to upgrade the Cake per seconds
  3. A Small Shop where you can choose and buy between 3 Skins
  4. A way to navigate between all of the pages
